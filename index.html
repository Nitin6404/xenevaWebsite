Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about XenevaOS

What is XenevaOS?

XenevaOS is an AR-native operating system built for next-gen computing. Designed from the ground up with our own kernel, it provides a seamless blend of augmented reality, AI integration, and immersive spatial interactions. Unlike traditional operating systems, XenevaOS enables holographic interfaces and real-time 3D environments.

How does XenevaOS work?

XenevaOS runs on AR-enabled devices, integrating gesture control, spatial UI, and AI-driven interactions. It supports wireless casting to external AR displays and native execution on standalone AR headsets. The system combines environment mapping, spatial awareness, and holographic rendering to create immersive computing experiences.

Who is XenevaOS for?

XenevaOS is for developers, innovators, and tech enthusiasts looking to build applications for AR glasses, smart environments, and spatial computing. Whether you're a software engineer, AI researcher, or XR designer, this is your playground for creating next-generation AR experiences.

Can XenevaOS be customized for different industries?

Yes! XenevaOS is designed as a universal platform that can be tailored to various industries and applications. While the core OS remains the same, it can be adapted to meet specific needs for education, healthcare, enterprise, or beyond. Its modular architecture allows organizations to implement custom features, optimized workflows, and unique user experiences without needing a completely different system.

Is XenevaOS open source?

Yes! XenevaOS is an open-source project, meaning developers worldwide can contribute, modify, and build upon its core technology. Our open-source approach accelerates innovation, fosters community collaboration, and ensures the platform evolves to meet real-world needs across industries.

When will XenevaOS be available?

According to our roadmap, the first prototype release is scheduled for Q2 2025, with the public beta planned for Q4 2025. Developers and early adopters will be able to access the platform during these phases. Full public release with ecosystem support is planned for 2026. Join our mailing list or developer community to stay updated.
